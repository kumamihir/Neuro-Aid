# Neuro-Aid: Smart Patient Safety System

## Overview

Neuro-Aid is a real-time monitoring and emergency response system designed for vulnerable patients, especially those suffering from Alzheimer’s and dementia. It provides an unobtrusive, holistic digital safety net to help reduce the risks of unmonitored care for elderly individuals, delivering peace of mind to carers and family members.

---

## Why Neuro-Aid?


Conventional care practices often leave gaps that increase risks for both patients and carers:

- **Carer Burnout:** Continuous anxiety and the impossibility of 24/7 manual supervision cause chronic stress.
- **Wandering Risk:** Dementia and Alzheimer’s patients may leave safe zones, stepping into dangerous situations.
- **Undetected Danger:** Lack of continuous, location-aware tracking leaves vulnerable patients alone.
- **Delayed Response:** Carers may not realize incidents have occurred until much later, worsening outcomes.

---

## Who Benefits?

- **Elderly Individuals:** Unobtrusive, continuous safety monitoring to preserve independence and wellbeing.
- **Alzheimer's & Dementia Patients:** Creates a crucial safety net for those at high risk of cognitive disorientation and wandering.
- **Carers & Families:** Empowers carers and family with instant visibility and automated alerts, for greater peace of mind.

---

## How It Works



- **Real-Time Geofencing:** Uses browser geolocation to continuously check patient location against pre-defined safe zones.
- **Automatic Zone Alerts:** Immediately triggers a response if a boundary is crossed — no manual action needed.
- **Twilio SMS Escalation:** Sends automated alert messages to registered carers, even without internet access.
- **Emergency SOS:** Manual trigger lets patients or bystanders instantly escalate an alert to all contacts.
